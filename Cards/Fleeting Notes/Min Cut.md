# Min Cut
The minimum sum of weights of edges that when removed, makes a [[Cut]] between $s$ and $t$ .

![](https://i.imgur.com/lnDLbd4.png)

In this case, the Min Cut has a total value of 9 + 5 + 2 + 4 = 20.

Used in [[Max-Flow Min-Cut Theorem]]
